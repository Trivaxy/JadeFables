The Everjade Mod is a mod focused on adding a biome, the Springs, that fits into vanilla's overall progression. The mod adds: 
61 Items
8 NPCs
1 biome 
3 New Structures

WARNING: This mod is multiplayer incompatible. Also content is only designed for prehardmode at the moment.
